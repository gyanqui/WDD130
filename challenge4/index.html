London is home to several iconic bridges, but perhaps the most famous is the Tower Bridge. This magnificent structure spans the River Thames and is instantly recognizable with its distinctive twin towers and suspension chains. Tower Bridge was completed in 1894 and has become a symbol of London's industrial heritage and engineering prowess. It's also a popular tourist attraction, with visitors able to take a walk across the high-level walkways and learn about the bridge's history and operation in the Tower Bridge Exhibition. Additionally, the bridge provides an important transport link for commuters and vehicles traveling between the north and south sides of the city. Overall, the Tower Bridge is a true marvel of engineering and an integral part of London's cultural and historical landscape.
